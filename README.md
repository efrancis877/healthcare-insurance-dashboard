# 📊 Healthcare Insurance Cost Analysis

This project analyzes the impact of lifestyle factors, specifically **BMI and smoking status**, on healthcare insurance costs using Power BI.

---

## 🔍 Objective

To identify key drivers of insurance cost variation and present insights through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- KPI cards displaying:
  - Average insurance cost  
  - Average BMI  
- Bar chart comparing insurance costs by smoking status  
- Scatter plot showing the relationship between BMI and insurance charges  
- Interactive slicers for filtering by region, smoking status, and sex  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Smokers incur significantly higher insurance costs compared to non-smokers.  
- Higher BMI shows a positive relationship with increased insurance charges.  
- Smoking status and BMI are key drivers of variation in insurance pricing.
